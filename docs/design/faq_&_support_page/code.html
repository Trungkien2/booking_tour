Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my booking for a full refund?

expand_more
Most tours offer a full refund if canceled at least 24 hours in advance of the start date. However, some special events or multi-day tours may have stricter policies. Please check the specific "Cancellation Policy" section on your tour's booking page for exact details.

What happens if it rains during the tour?

expand_more
Our tours generally operate rain or shine! We recommend checking the forecast and dressing appropriately. In the event of extreme weather conditions where safety is a concern, the tour operator may cancel. In such cases, you will be offered a rescheduling option or a full refund.

Do you offer hotel pick-up services?

expand_more
Pick-up services vary by tour. Many of our day trips include hotel pick-up and drop-off from select downtown locations. You can see if "Hotel Pickup" is included in the "Inclusions" section of the tour description. If not, a central meeting point will be provided.

How do I receive my tickets?

expand_more
Once your booking is confirmed, you will receive an email with a voucher. This voucher serves as your ticket. You can present it on your mobile device or print it out to show the guide at the meeting point.
