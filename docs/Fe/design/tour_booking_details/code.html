About this tour

Immerse yourself in the breathtaking beauty of the Norwegian fjords on this 3-day guided kayaking adventure. Starting from the historic village of Geiranger, we'll paddle through crystal-clear waters, past thundering waterfalls like the Seven Sisters and the Suitor.

Nights will be spent in comfortable wilderness camps or cozy mountain cabins, where you can relax by the fire and share stories. Whether you're a seasoned paddler or a beginner, our expert guides ensure a safe and unforgettable journey into the heart of nature.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & First Paddle

Meet at Geiranger harbor at 09:00 AM. Safety briefing and equipment fitting. Paddle to Skageflå farm for a lunch break and hike.

Day 2: Deep Fjord Exploration

Full day of kayaking deep into the fjord arms where large ships cannot go. Spot local wildlife including eagles and porpoises.

Day 3: Return & Farewell

Morning paddle back towards civilization. Final group lunch and debriefing. Tour concludes approx 3:00 PM.
